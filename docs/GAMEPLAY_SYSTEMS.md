# Gameplay Systems

## Character System
- Abilities: STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHA.
- Proficiency bonus scales by level.
- Core actions: attack, ability checks, saving throws, rest recovery.

## Combat System
- Turn order by initiative.
- Attack resolution: d20 roll + modifiers vs AC.
- Damage resolution and HP tracking.
- Conditions (stunned, poisoned, prone, etc.) influence outcomes.

## Exploration and Travel
- Travel pace by terrain.
- Encounter chance influenced by region type.
- Dungeon nodes as high-risk/high-reward zones.

## Progression
- XP milestones and level advancement.
- Class identity through subclass and role behavior.
- Equipment and rewards from chapter and dungeon completion.

## Quest System
- Main quest drives chapter completion.
- Side quests deepen regional stakes and rewards.
- Quest states: active/completed/failed (extensible).

## Faction System
- Reputation values per faction.
- Unlocks can gate story variants, shop options, and support assets.
