# World & Story Bible

## Campaign Premise
A failing planar seal causes cascading crises across 12 kingdoms. The player party unifies fractured powers while confronting cults, warlords, and extraplanar entities.

## Structural Canon
- 12 Acts.
- 50 Chapters total.
- 1 Main Quest per chapter.
- 2 Side Quests per chapter.

## Story Escalation Pattern
- Acts 1-3: Local crisis and political positioning.
- Acts 4-6: Regional warfare and betrayal.
- Acts 7-9: Planar threat emergence and kingdom-scale conflict.
- Acts 10-12: Apocalyptic convergence and final oath resolution.

## Region Composition
- 3 continents, 6 countries, 12 kingdoms.
- 9 cities per kingdom.
- Dungeons and underground zones linked to chapter arcs.

## Villain Ladder
- Tier 1: Local warbands and cult cells.
- Tier 2: Regional commanders and corrupted nobility.
- Tier 3: Legendary entities and planar lieutenants.
- Tier 4: Campaign final antagonist.
